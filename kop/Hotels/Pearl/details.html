Hotel Pearl, Kolhapur’s most prestigious hotel, was established in the year 1971. It has luxuriously appointed 30 twin bedded guest rooms which have been meticulously planned with a host of modern amenities. It is an ISO 9001:2008 certified hotel. Situated close to the railway station and central bus stand and about 30 minutes drive from the airport, the hotel enjoys an excellent location and serene surroundings. Hotel Pearl is the first choice of travellers visiting Kolhapur and those who stop in transit.
